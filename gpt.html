We, the developers, do not store, track, or transmit any personal or message data to our servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

3. OpenAI API & Message Handling

Message processing is conducted via the OpenAI API:

  • User prompts are sent to OpenAI servers.

Your OpenAI API key is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services. We recommend you take steps to keep it secure.
